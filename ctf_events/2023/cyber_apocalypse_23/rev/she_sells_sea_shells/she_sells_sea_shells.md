So it's like this:

-   our 77 byte input is XORd with `m1`
-   the output is compared with `t`
-   if it matches, our input is XORd with `m2`
-   the result is our flag

Plan of action:

-   XOR `t` with `m2` to recover out `input` (plaintext)
